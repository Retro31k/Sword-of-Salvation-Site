Gameplay & World

Simple but Engaging Combat System

Engage in deadly battles where every missed strike matters. Choose your playstyle, combine abilities, and master the art of fighting demon hordes.

  • Real-time combat with weapon combos, dodging and parrying
  • Various weapon combinations, summonable creatures and magic runes
  • Epic battles with a variety of bosses

Dark Fantasy World

Immerse yourself in a rich, atmospheric world filled with the secrets of the gods, forgotten civilizations and dark magic. Every location holds terrifying secrets.

  • Many unique biomes
  • Atmospheric dungeons
  • Various types of monsters and demons

Simple Progression System

Discover new weapons, find companions, and collect magic runes to customize your playstyle.

  • Several weapon types — choose what you like
  • ✦ Simple and clear leveling
  • Separate mode for quick grinding and testing your skills
